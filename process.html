01
Phase 01Discovery & Audit

We listen, learn, and diagnose.

Timeline: Week 1–2

Before we write a single ad or send a single email, we do the work that most agencies skip: deep discovery. We analyse everything — your current marketing performance, your competitors, your audience, and your business model — to understand exactly where your growth opportunities lie and what's been holding you back.

This phase produces a clear, documented picture of your starting position and the strategic rationale for everything we'll build together.

Kickoff Call & Questionnaire
Structured discovery session covering your goals, audiences, competitive landscape, current challenges, and definition of success.
Advertising Account Audit
Full audit of existing Google Ads, Meta, LinkedIn, TikTok, and email accounts — identifying wasted spend, missed opportunities, and structural issues.
Competitor & Market Analysis
Mapping the competitive landscape: who is advertising, on which channels, with what messaging — and where the gaps are you can exploit.
Audit Report & Findings Presentation
A clear, jargon-free report documenting everything we found, our interpretation of the data, and our initial strategic recommendations.
02
Phase 02Strategy & Architecture

We design the blueprint.

Timeline: Week 2–3

Armed with the insights from Discovery, we design a full-funnel growth strategy tailored to your goals, budget, and competitive position. This is where we make the big decisions: which channels, which audiences, which messages, and which metrics matter.

The strategy document we produce isn't a slide deck of vague recommendations — it's an actionable blueprint with campaign architectures, audience maps, creative briefs, and KPI frameworks.

Channel & Budget Strategy
Recommending the optimal channel mix and budget allocation based on your audience, goals, and competitive landscape.
Campaign Architecture Design
Building the full campaign and ad set structure, targeting layers, and bidding strategy before a single campaign goes live.
Audience Research & Persona Mapping
Building detailed audience segments for each platform, including custom audiences, lookalikes, intent signals, and exclusion lists.
Creative Brief & KPI Framework
Defining the creative direction, messaging hierarchy, and the specific KPIs we'll be held accountable to — agreed upfront with you.
03
Phase 03Build & Launch

We build, verify, and go live.

Timeline: Week 4

With the strategy approved, we move into build mode — setting up every campaign, automation, creative, and tracking element with precision. Nothing goes live until it's been quality-checked against our 47-point pre-launch checklist.

We handle everything: account access, pixel setup, creative production, copy approval, tracking verification. You don't need to manage the technical details — that's what we're here for.

Campaign Build & Creative Production
Building all campaigns, ad sets, ads, email sequences, and creative assets ready for approval in a structured creative review process.
Tracking & Attribution Setup
Installing and verifying all conversion tracking: Google Tag Manager, Meta Pixel, LinkedIn Insight Tag, and UTM framework.
Pre-Launch QA & Client Approval
Walking through every campaign element with you before launch — nothing goes live without your sign-off.
Go Live & First-Week Monitoring
Launching all campaigns simultaneously and monitoring hourly in the first 72 hours to catch any technical issues early.
04
Phase 04Optimise & Report

We improve, always.

Timeline: Ongoing

This is where most agencies stop, but where we're just getting started. Continuous optimisation is the difference between a campaign that works in month one and a campaign that doubles performance by month six.

You'll always know exactly what's happening — weekly optimisation updates, monthly strategy reviews, and a real-time dashboard that you can access 24/7. No waiting for a monthly report to understand your results.

Weekly Performance Optimisation
Bid adjustments, budget reallocation, audience pruning, creative refresh, and keyword management — every week, without exception.
A/B Testing Programme
Systematic testing of ad copy, creative formats, landing page elements, email subject lines — building a compounding competitive advantage.
Monthly Strategy Review
A dedicated call reviewing the month's performance, insights, learnings, and the strategic plan for the month ahead — with you, not at you.
Real-Time Dashboard Access
Your personalised Looker Studio dashboard shows live spend, impressions, clicks, conversions, and ROAS — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
