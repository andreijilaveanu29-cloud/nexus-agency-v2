Our Process

A repeatable four-phase system built to deliver consistent results — from first conversation to continuous optimisation. No guesswork, no surprises.

01
Phase 01Discovery & Audit

We listen, learn, and diagnose.

Week 1–2

Before we write a single ad, we do the work most agencies skip: deep discovery. We analyse your current marketing, competitors, and audience to understand exactly where your growth opportunities lie.

Kickoff Call & Questionnaire
Structured discovery session covering goals, audiences, competitive landscape, and definition of success.
Advertising Account Audit
Full audit of existing Google Ads, Meta, LinkedIn, TikTok, and email accounts — identifying wasted spend and missed opportunities.
Competitor & Market Analysis
Mapping the competitive landscape: who is advertising, on which channels, and where the gaps are you can exploit.
Audit Report & Findings Presentation
A clear, jargon-free report documenting everything we found with strategic recommendations.
02
Phase 02Strategy & Architecture

We design the blueprint.

Week 2–3

Armed with insights from Discovery, we design a full-funnel growth strategy tailored to your goals, budget, and competitive position. This is where we decide which channels, audiences, messages, and metrics matter.

Channel & Budget Strategy
Recommending the optimal channel mix and budget allocation based on your audience and goals.
Campaign Architecture Design
Building the full campaign structure, targeting layers, and bidding strategy before a single campaign goes live.
Audience Research & Persona Mapping
Building detailed audience segments for each platform, including custom audiences, lookalikes, and exclusion lists.
Creative Brief & KPI Framework
Defining the creative direction and the specific KPIs we'll be held accountable to — agreed upfront with you.
03
Phase 03Build & Launch

We build, verify, and go live.

Week 4

With the strategy approved, we move into build mode — setting up every campaign, automation, creative, and tracking element with precision. Nothing goes live until it's been quality-checked against our pre-launch checklist.

Campaign Build & Creative Production
Building all campaigns, ad sets, ads, email sequences, and creative assets ready for your approval.
Tracking & Attribution Setup
Installing and verifying all conversion tracking: Google Tag Manager, Meta Pixel, LinkedIn Insight Tag.
Pre-Launch QA & Client Approval
Walking through every campaign element with you before launch — nothing goes live without your sign-off.
Go Live & First-Week Monitoring
Launching all campaigns and monitoring hourly in the first 72 hours to catch any issues early.
04
Phase 04Optimise & Report

We improve, always.

Ongoing

This is where most agencies stop, but where we're just getting started. Continuous optimisation is the difference between a campaign that works in month one and one that doubles performance by month six.

Weekly Performance Optimisation
Bid adjustments, budget reallocation, audience pruning, creative refresh — every week without exception.
A/B Testing Programme
Systematic testing of ad copy, creative formats, landing page elements, and email subject lines.
Monthly Strategy Review
A dedicated call reviewing performance, insights, and the strategic plan for the month ahead — with you, not at you.
Real-Time Dashboard Access
Your personalised dashboard shows live spend, conversions, and ROAS — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
