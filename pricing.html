Common Questions

Pricing FAQs

Is the advertising budget included in the retainer?
No — the retainer covers our management fee. Ad spend is paid directly to the platforms (Google, Meta, etc.) and is on top of the retainer. We recommend a minimum ad budget based on your goals during the audit.
Is there a minimum contract length?
We ask for an initial 3-month commitment to allow campaigns to optimise and deliver meaningful results. After that, contracts roll monthly. We believe in earning your business every month, not locking you in.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Absolutely. We review your plan quarterly and adjust based on your growth, budget, and changing priorities. Many clients start on Starter and graduate to Growth as they scale.
What happens to my ad accounts if I leave?
All ad accounts, creative assets, tracking setups, and data belong to you — not us. We work inside your owned accounts from day one. Everything we build is yours to keep.
Do you work with businesses outside Poland?
Yes — we work with clients across Europe and internationally. Our team is fully remote and we work across all time zones. Client communication is in English, German, Romanian, or Polish.
How quickly can we get started?
After the free audit, most clients are fully onboarded and campaigns are live within 4 weeks. For urgent projects or quick-launch needs, we can often move faster.
