Common Questions

Pricing FAQs

Is ad spend included in the retainer?
No — the retainer covers our management fee. Ad spend is paid directly to the platforms (Google, Meta, etc.) and is separate. We recommend a minimum budget based on your goals during the audit.
Is there a minimum contract length?
We ask for an initial 3-month commitment to allow campaigns to optimise. After that, contracts roll monthly. We earn your business every month, not by locking you in.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Absolutely. We review your plan quarterly and adjust based on your growth and priorities. Many clients start on Starter and graduate to Growth as they scale.
What happens to my accounts if I leave?
All ad accounts, creative assets, tracking setups, and data belong to you — not us. We work inside your owned accounts from day one. Everything we build is yours to keep.
Do you work internationally?
Yes — we serve clients across Europe, North America, and beyond. Our team is fully remote and we work across all time zones.
How quickly can we get started?
After the free audit, most clients are onboarded and live within 4 weeks. For urgent projects, we can often move faster.
